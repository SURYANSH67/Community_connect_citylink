Privacy & Security

How we protect your data and maintain trust

1. Information Collection

When you report an issue via CityLink Connect, we collect the necessary information to resolve the problem effectively. This may include the issue description, geographic coordinates, photographic evidence, and optionally, your name, email, and phone number to provide status updates. We do not collect unnecessary sensitive personal data.

2. Data Usage & Sharing

The information collected is used strictly for platform demonstration—to review, categorize, and manage civic complaints within the system. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared with designated platform administrators solely for the purpose of demonstrating the resolution workflow.

3. Security Measures

CityLink Connect implements robust technical security measures to protect against unauthorized access or alteration of user data. This includes secure HTTPS encryption for all data transport, encrypted password storage using modern hashing algorithms, and strict access controls for administrative dashboards.

4. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to request the deletion of your account. Note that removing your account may anonymize previously submitted test complaints rather than delete them entirely, to maintain the operational history of the project.
