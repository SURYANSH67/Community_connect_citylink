Accessibility Policy

Our Commitment to an Inclusive Civic Platform

Our Commitment

The Community Connect academic project is committed to ensuring that our digital interfaces are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We continually strive to improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards.

Accessibility Features

To enhance user accessibility, CityLink Connect implements the following features:

  • High Contrast Mode: A dedicated toggle is available in the navigation bar to switch the website theme for users with visual impairments.
  • Keyboard Navigation: All essential features and forms can be navigated using a keyboard.
  • Screen Reader Support: We utilize ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) labels and structural HTML5 elements to ensure compatibility with screen readers.
  • Responsive Design: The platform is fully usable on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

Feedback and Support

We are continuously improving the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any barriers or have recommendations for improvement, please contact our support team.
