Reporting Process Workflow

1. Submit Request

Provide accurate location details, a clear description, and photographic evidence of the issue using our secure online form.

2. City Review

Your report receives a tracking ID and is routed to the corresponding system department module for assessment and scheduling.

3. Resolution

Track progress online. City crews will address the issue prioritized by safety impact and available resources.
